Scratch Projects

Scratch Reflection: The purpose of the game Lab Rat (green screenshot) is to have the subject reach the end of the maze alive. You take damage when an enemy touches you. The game mostly consists of broadcasts and if/then statements. For example, if we want the player to take damage, then the enemy touches the player. The most challenging part was finding a sprite that is small enough to fit the maze, but big enough for the player to see. We decided to use a red dot to represent the test subject because it stands out.
